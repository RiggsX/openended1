# Weekly Planning & Review System

**Tier:** Core  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Target:** Anyone who wants to be more intentional about how they spend their time

---

## What This Does

A simple weekly planning and review system that helps you prioritize what matters, track progress, and continuously improve. Takes 30 minutes on Sunday, 10 minutes daily.

---

## Execution Flow

### Step 1: Weekly Review (Sunday Evening)
**Trigger:** Every Sunday at 6 PM (or your preferred time)

**Prompt:**
```
Review the past week:

# Weekly Review - Week of [Date]

## Last Week's Wins
What went well? (List 3-5 things you're proud of)
1. [Win 1]
2. [Win 2]
3. [Win 3]

## Last Week's Challenges
What didn't go as planned? (Be honest, no judgment)
1. [Challenge 1]
2. [Challenge 2]
3. [Challenge 3]

## Lessons Learned
What did you learn this week?
- [Lesson 1]
- [Lesson 2]

## Incomplete Tasks
What didn't get done? (And why?)
- [Task 1] - Reason: [Why it didn't happen]
- [Task 2] - Reason: [Why it didn't happen]

**Decision:** 
- [ ] Carry forward (still important)
- [ ] Delegate (someone else should do this)
- [ ] Delete (no longer relevant)
- [ ] Defer (not urgent, schedule for later)

## Energy & Well-being
**Energy level this week:** [1-10]
**Sleep quality:** [Good/Fair/Poor]
**Exercise:** [Days active]
**Stress level:** [1-10]

**What affected your energy:**
- [Factor 1]
- [Factor 2]

**What would help next week:**
- [Action 1]
- [Action 2]
```

**Decision Point:** If energy level is <5 for 2+ weeks → prioritize rest and recovery over productivity.

### Step 2: Weekly Planning (Sunday Evening)
**Prompt:**
```
Plan the upcoming week:

# Weekly Plan - Week of [Date]

## Theme of the Week
[One word or phrase that captures your focus]
Example: "Execution", "Learning", "Connection", "Recovery"

## Top 3 Priorities
What MUST get done this week? (Only 3!)
1. [Priority 1]
   - Why it matters: [Impact]
   - Success looks like: [Specific outcome]
   - Time needed: [Hours]

2. [Priority 2]
   - Why it matters: [Impact]
   - Success looks like: [Specific outcome]
   - Time needed: [Hours]

3. [Priority 3]
   - Why it matters: [Impact]
   - Success looks like: [Specific outcome]
   - Time needed: [Hours]

## Secondary Tasks
What would be nice to do? (5-7 tasks)
- [ ] [Task 1]
- [ ] [Task 2]
- [ ] [Task 3]
- [ ] [Task 4]
- [ ] [Task 5]

## Time Blocks
**Monday:**
- Morning: [Focus block for Priority 1]
- Afternoon: [Meetings/Secondary tasks]
- Evening: [Personal time]

**Tuesday:**
- Morning: [Focus block for Priority 2]
- Afternoon: [Meetings/Secondary tasks]
- Evening: [Personal time]

**Wednesday:**
- Morning: [Focus block for Priority 3]
- Afternoon: [Meetings/Secondary tasks]
- Evening: [Personal time]

**Thursday:**
- Morning: [Focus block or catch-up]
- Afternoon: [Meetings/Secondary tasks]
- Evening: [Personal time]

**Friday:**
- Morning: [Wrap-up and planning]
- Afternoon: [Light work/admin]
- Evening: [Weekend transition]

**Weekend:**
- Saturday: [Rest/Personal projects]
- Sunday: [Review and planning]

## Meetings & Commitments
**Scheduled:**
- [Meeting 1] - [Day/Time]
- [Meeting 2] - [Day/Time]

**To Schedule:**
- [Meeting 3] - [Who to contact]

## Potential Obstacles
What could derail your plan?
1. [Obstacle 1] - Mitigation: [How you'll handle it]
2. [Obstacle 2] - Mitigation: [How you'll handle it]

## Self-Care Non-Negotiables
- [ ] Exercise: [X days, Y minutes]
- [ ] Sleep: [X hours per night]
- [ ] Social: [Connect with X people]
- [ ] Downtime: [X hours of rest]
```

**Decision Point:** If you have >3 top priorities → you don't have priorities. Cut it down to 3.

### Step 3: Daily Check-In (Every Morning)
**Prompt:**
```
Start your day with intention:

# Daily Check-In - [Day, Date]

## Today's Focus
What's the ONE thing that would make today a success?
[Your most important task]

## Today's Schedule
**Morning (8-12):**
- [Time block 1]
- [Time block 2]

**Afternoon (12-5):**
- [Time block 3]
- [Time block 4]

**Evening (5-9):**
- [Personal time]

## Top 3 Tasks
1. [ ] [Task 1] - Priority: High
2. [ ] [Task 2] - Priority: Medium
3. [ ] [Task 3] - Priority: Medium

## Meetings Today
- [Meeting 1] - [Time] - Prep needed: [Yes/No]
- [Meeting 2] - [Time] - Prep needed: [Yes/No]

## Potential Distractions
What might pull you off track today?
- [Distraction 1] - Plan: [How you'll avoid it]
- [Distraction 2] - Plan: [How you'll avoid it]

## Energy Management
**Current energy:** [1-10]
**Peak hours:** [When you're most productive]
**Plan:** [Schedule hard tasks during peak hours]

## Mindset
**Intention for today:** [How you want to show up]
Example: "Patient", "Focused", "Creative", "Collaborative"

---

Time needed: 5-10 minutes
```

## Quality Standards

✅ **Success Criteria:**
- Weekly review completed every Sunday (90%+ consistency)
- Daily check-ins take <10 minutes
- Top 3 priorities are clear and specific
- At least 2 of 3 weekly priorities completed
- You feel more in control of your time
- Energy and well-being are tracked and improving

❌ **Failure Modes:**
- Skipping reviews (system breaks down)
- Too many priorities (everything is important = nothing is important)
- Not being honest in reviews (defeats the purpose)
- Planning without executing
- Beating yourself up for incomplete tasks
- Making it too complicated (should be simple)

## Usage Notes

- **Start simple:** Just do weekly review and planning for the first month
- **Be consistent:** Same time every week (build the habit)
- **Be honest:** This is for you, not for show
- **Adjust as needed:** Customize the prompts to fit your life
- **Celebrate wins:** Acknowledge progress, even small wins
- **Be kind to yourself:** Some weeks will be better than others
