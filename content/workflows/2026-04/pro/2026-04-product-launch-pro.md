# Product Launch Framework: Go-to-Market Execution

**Tier:** Pro  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Target:** Product managers and marketing leaders launching new products or major features

---

## What This Does

A comprehensive framework for planning and executing product launches‚Äîfrom positioning to post-launch analysis. Ensures nothing falls through the cracks and maximizes launch impact.

---

## Framework Overview

This is a **12-week launch framework** with clear phases and deliverables.

**Timeline:**
- Weeks 1-4: Foundation (positioning, messaging, strategy)
- Weeks 5-8: Preparation (assets, enablement, partnerships)
- Weeks 9-10: Pre-launch (buzz building, early access)
- Week 11: Launch week
- Week 12: Post-launch optimization

---
